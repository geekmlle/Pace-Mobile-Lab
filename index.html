Pace Mobile App Design Contest 4.0/Hackathon

The Pace Mobile App Design Contest 4.0 is based on the popular hackathon. Participants form teams to create a concept mobile application using any tool (e.g., Proto.io) or a scripting/programming language (e.g., HTML5) and demonstrate it to the audience and the judging panel. Prizes are awarded based on judges' expert opinion.
